Where I am from

I was born in Jingdezhen, Jiangxi Province, China, a historic city known as the "Porcelain Capital" of China. My hometown has a rich tradition of producing ceramics, dating back to ancient times when it manufactured porcelain exclusively for the emperors. In fact, the blue and white porcelain(Deftware) that is widely known in the Netherlands was originally invented in my hometown. Besides our famous ceramics, Jingdezhen is also known across China for its spicy food. Many of the local dishes are even spicier than the famous Sichuan hot pot.

My Experience

I joined ASML in June 2024. Prior to joining the company, I had lived in Canada for two years and had a background in Computer Engineering. Given my experience and expertise, my manager believed that I would adapt well to the European living environment and quickly learn the VCP-related knowledge. As a result, I was assigned to take the PL role here for a three-month period.

Prior to going to Canada, I spent two years working at YMTC, where I focused on the research and development of CMOS devices. During my time there, I was actively involved in the development of both the 64L and 128L generations of products.

My Hobbies

Outside of work, I enjoy hiking, playing basketball, gaming, and cooking. Although programming is not currently my main profession, I have a passion for it and often work on small programming projects in my spare time for personal enjoyment.

I enjoyed cooking Chinese dishes on weekends and inviting friends over to share meals. We would often spend time chatting, playing board games, or gaming together, which was a great way to unwind and connect with others.
